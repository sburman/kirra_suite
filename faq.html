Good to know

Frequently asked questions

The quick answers, and where to book for the best rate.

Where is The Kirra Suite?

On Level 8, Marine Parade, Kirra (Coolangatta) QLD 4225, directly across the road from Kirra Beach on the southern Gold Coast.

How many guests can stay?

Up to four. There's a master king bedroom plus a second bedroom with a plush queen sofa bed, ideal for couples, families or friends travelling together.

How many bedrooms and bathrooms?

Two bedrooms and one bathroom. The master king bedroom and the second bedroom share an adjoining bathroom.

Is there parking?

Yes, one complimentary secure car space is included. There's also a private storage cage in the secure car park, ideal for surfboards, bicycles and e-bikes.

Is there Wi-Fi and TV?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi and three Smart TVs, one in the living area and one in each bedroom.

What kitchen and laundry facilities are provided?

A fully equipped kitchen with a coffee machine, plus a concealed laundry with washing machine, dryer and drying rack.

Can guests use the pool and gym?

Yes, guests enjoy access to the building's premium resident facilities, including the resort-style pool, sun deck and fully equipped gym.

Is the beachfront BBQ available?

The beachfront BBQ and entertaining area may be available to book on request through your host.

How far is the airport?

Gold Coast Airport (OOL) is roughly a ten-minute drive away, one of the easiest beachfront stays to reach on the coast.

How do I get the best price?

Book direct through POD Property Management for the lowest available rate with no platform fees. The apartment is also on Airbnb and Booking.com.

Is The Kirra Suite good for families?

Yes. It sleeps up to four with a king master bedroom and a second bedroom with a queen sofa bed, and patrolled, family-friendly Kirra Beach is right across the road.

Can I walk to cafés and restaurants?

Yes. Cafés, restaurants, bakeries and beachfront bars are an easy stroll away, with plenty more along the nearby Coolangatta strip. See the area guide for local favourites.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3pm (15:00) and check-out is by 10am. Early check-in or late check-out may be available on request, subject to availability — ask your host when you book.

Are pets allowed?

No, pets are not permitted at The Kirra Suite.

Is there air conditioning?

Yes, the apartment has ducted air conditioning throughout, keeping it comfortable in Queensland's summer heat.

What linen, towels and toiletries are provided?

All linen and towels are provided and the apartment is fully made up on arrival. Essential toiletries are supplied. Just bring your holiday clothes.

What streaming services are available?

The three Smart TVs run Google TV. You can sign in to your own subscriptions — Netflix, Disney+, Stan, YouTube and more — during your stay. No accounts are pre-installed on the televisions.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms depend on your booking channel and the rate selected at checkout. Policies differ between POD Property Management (direct), Airbnb and Booking.com — check the cancellation terms shown when you book.

Who manages The Kirra Suite?

The Kirra Suite is professionally managed by POD Property Management, a local Gold Coast agency. Your host is Camille, a verified Airbnb Superhost with 10+ years of hosting experience, 1,100+ reviews and a 4.9-star rating across her Gold Coast properties. Direct bookings through POD receive the best available rate and personal host support.

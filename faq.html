Frequently asked questions

The quick answers — and where to book for the best rate.

Where is The Kirra Suite?

On Level 8 of Miles, Marine Parade, Kirra Point, Kirra (Coolangatta) QLD 4225 — directly across the road from Kirra Beach on the southern Gold Coast.

How many guests can stay?

Up to four. There's a master king bedroom plus a second bedroom with a plush queen sofa bed — ideal for couples, families or friends travelling together.

How many bedrooms and bathrooms?

Two bedrooms and one bathroom. The master king bedroom and the second bedroom share an adjoining bathroom.

Is there parking?

Yes — one complimentary secure car space is included. There's also a private storage cage in the secure car park, ideal for surfboards, bicycles and e-bikes.

Is there Wi-Fi and TV?

Yes — high-speed Wi-Fi and three Smart TVs, one in the living area and one in each bedroom.

What kitchen and laundry facilities are provided?

A fully equipped kitchen with a coffee machine, plus a concealed laundry with washing machine, dryer and drying rack.

Can guests use the pool and gym?

Yes — guests enjoy access to the building's premium resident facilities, including the resort-style pool, sun deck and fully equipped gym.

Is the beachfront BBQ available?

The beachfront BBQ and entertaining area may be available to book on request through your host.

How far is the airport?

Gold Coast Airport (OOL) is roughly a ten-minute drive away — one of the easiest beachfront stays to reach on the coast.

How do I get the best price?

Book direct through POD Property Management for the lowest available rate with no platform fees. The apartment is also on Airbnb and Booking.com.

The Suite

Calm, coastal and beautifully considered

Set high on Level 8 of a prestigious beachfront building in Kirra, The Kirra Suite is a light-filled apartment styled with brand-new designer furnishings for comfort, calm and luxury. The open-plan living, dining and kitchen space flows out to a private balcony with sweeping ocean views over Kirra Beach — the perfect spot for your morning coffee or evening drink.

The master king bedroom and a second bedroom with a plush queen sofa bed share an adjoining bathroom, with a television in each room — an easy layout for couples, families or friends travelling together. A fully equipped kitchen with coffee machine, concealed laundry, three Smart TVs and a welcome hamper on arrival mean everything's been thought of, whether you're here for a weekend escape or a longer coastal stay.

The Building

Resort-style living by Kirra Beach

The Kirra Suite sits within a striking beachfront residence with a landscaped lobby, resort-style pool and sun deck, and a fully equipped gym, all just an elevator ride away.

Step outside and you're moments from the Kirra Beach Hotel, beachfront Kirra Beach House and Made Espresso Bar, with Kirra's surf clubs, the scenic coastal pathway and golden sand only steps away.

The Suite

Breezy coastal days, beautifully easy

Set high on Level 8 of a prestigious beachfront building in Kirra, The Kirra Suite is a light-filled apartment dressed in brand-new designer furnishings that feel calm, breezy and effortless. The open-plan living, dining and kitchen spill out to a private balcony with sweeping ocean views over Kirra Beach, the perfect place for a slow morning coffee or a wine as the sun drops.

The master king room and a second bedroom with a plush queen sofa bed share an adjoining bathroom, with a TV in each, an easy fit for couples, families or friends. A full kitchen with coffee machine, a concealed laundry, three Smart TVs and a welcome hamper on arrival mean it's all taken care of. Just unpack, kick off your shoes and settle in, whether it's a weekend away or a long, lazy stay.

The Building

Resort-style living by Kirra Beach

The Kirra Suite sits within a striking beachfront residence with a landscaped lobby, resort-style pool and sun deck, and a fully equipped gym, all just an elevator ride away.

Step outside and you're moments from the Kirra Beach Hotel, beachfront Kirra Beach House and Made Espresso Bar, with Kirra's surf clubs, the scenic coastal pathway and golden sand only steps away.

The Suite

Calm, coastal and beautifully considered

Set high on Level 8 of the landmark Miles building at Kirra Point, The Kirra Suite is a light-filled apartment styled for easy coastal living. Designer furnishings, a full kitchen and an open-plan living space flow out to a private balcony with sweeping ocean views.

The master bedroom features a king bed, while the second room offers a queen sofa bed and media space — comfortably sleeping four. Three smart TVs, fast Wi-Fi, a concealed laundry and a private storage cage for boards and bikes make longer stays effortless.

The Building

Resort living at Miles, Kirra Point

The Kirra Suite sits within Miles — a striking beachfront residence with a landscaped lobby, resort-style pool and sun deck, and a fully equipped gym, all just an elevator ride away.

Downstairs, the Kirra Point precinct lives up to its "eat, drink, live & stay" promise — the Kirra Beach Hotel, beachfront Kirra Beach House and Made Espresso Bar are all at your door, with the scenic coastal pathway and golden sand only moments away.
